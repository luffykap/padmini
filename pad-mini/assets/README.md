## Design Guidelines for Assets

### App Icon
- Use soft, warm colors (pink/lavender)
- Include a simple flower or supportive symbol
- Ensure readability at small sizes
- Follow platform guidelines (iOS/Android)

### Splash Screen
- Light background with pastel colors
- Simple, welcoming design
- App logo/name
- Loading indicator space

### Colors to Use
- Primary: #e91e63 (soft pink)
- Secondary: #9c27b0 (lavender)
- Accent: #4dd0e1 (teal)
- Background: #fce4ec (very light pink)
